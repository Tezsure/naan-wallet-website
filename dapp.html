1/1 on Tezos - First week of July

The focus of the 1of1 event is to change the narrative around Tezos NFT's as being low priced, multi-edition only. Organized by a group of Tezos artists and collectors.

Mint during the first week of July, on any Tezos NFT platform, using the hashtag "#1of1".

Multiple events will be organized during July. Including galleries, dedicated Twitter Spaces for collectors and artists, and much more!

NFTBiker will provide a central page to view and collect #1of1 NFT art.
