What are DApps?

What is a Decentralized App (DApp)?

A Decentralized App (DApp) is a website or app that uses decentralized protocols for some or all aspect of its operation.

All DApps available on Naan are built on the Tezos blockchain. Tezos is a decentralized protocol capable of running smart contracts - computer code that is stored and run by a decentralized network of computers around the world.

DApps powered by smart contracts that are deployed on chain with verifiable source code are trustless and uncensorable because it is run in a deterministic and distributed fashion by computers around the world.

What can I do with DApps?

The wide variety of DApps already launched allow you to do different things - exchange tokens trustlessly, buy and trade collectibles that live on the blockchain, earn money, make payments and more. The Naan team curates a list of popular and useful DApps.

What is "tez" and how do I obtain it?

Tez or XTZ is the main cryptocurrency on the Tezos blockchain. You need tez to make transactions and pay for transaction fees (gas) on the network. Tez can be bought through Naan wallet with a credit card or on all major cryptocurrency exchanges like Coinbase, Kraken, or Binance.

Once you own some tez, you can transfer tez to your Naan Wallet account by sending tez from your exchange account to your wallet address. What is gas?

Gas is the execution fee for every transaction made on the Tezos blockchain. Suggested gas fees rise and fall depending on the volume of transactions being made on the network.
